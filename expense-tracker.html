About Data Management

Your expense data is stored locally in your device's storage using IndexedDB. This means:

  • Your data stays on your device and is not sent to any server
  • Your data persists even when you close the browser
  • Your data has more storage capacity than regular browser storage
  • Your data won't sync between different devices or browsers

Backup Recommendations

To keep your data safe, we recommend:

  • Regularly export your data as a backup
  • Store backup files in a safe location (cloud storage, external drive, etc.)
  • Import your backup if you change browsers or devices
